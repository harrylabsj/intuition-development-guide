# Intuition Development Guide
Teaches systematic approaches to developing and trusting intuitive judgment over time.
## What it does
- Helps you identify your personal intuition "signature" — whether it arrives as physical sensation, emotional shift, sudden knowing, or persistent thought
- Provides a 3-Step Intuition Check for structured calibration before major decisions: define, scan bodily signals, note clarity
- Includes decision journaling practice and somatic awareness exercises to build a personal evidence base for intuitive accuracy
## Example scenarios
**1. 不知道怎么选对象**
> 👤 同时跟两个女生在接触，一个条件很好但没感觉，一个感觉很好但条件差，直觉告诉我要选有感觉的，但理性又觉得选条件好的，怎么办？
> 🤖 理性-直觉整合法分析：1）先用直觉做第一轮筛选——闭上眼睛想象和每个女生相处的5年后感受；2）再用理性做第二层评估——列出两段关系的核心矛盾（价值观差异、未来规划是否一致）；3）做'最难接受实验'——最坏情况发生10年，哪边你更愿意承受？建议不要被条件/感觉二选一框住。

**2. 学会信任直觉**
> 👤 "I overthink every decision. Teach me to access and trust my intuition."
> 🤖 Intuition-building program — body-signal awareness training (gut feelings, tension patterns mapped to decisions), intuition-vs-fear differentiation exercises, small-decision practice protocol (start with low-stakes lunches/books, log intuitive hits vs. misses), and decision journal template.

**3. 领导者直觉与数据的平衡**
> 👤 "In my leadership role, when should I trust gut feel vs. demand more data?"
> 🤖 Intuition-in-leadership framework — domain-expertise check (intuition reliable where you have 10,000+ hours of pattern recognition), decision-type taxonomy (people decisions → intuition-weighted, resource allocation → data-weighted), and 'intuition stress test' questions to pressure-test gut calls before acting.
